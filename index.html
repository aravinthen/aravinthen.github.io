Purpose

This is a living research log where I document papers I read, technical concepts I study, and progress on my own research projects.

The core of my research interests lie in deep reinforcement learning and optimization, specifically for complex adversarial and multi-agent scenarios. I started working in this field in November 2025 after a few years spent in computational physics research. In a way, what I write here is a reflection of my journey in mastering this new field.

Please note that the research that I document here has very little to do with my professional activities: this is what I do in my spare time outside of my work hours. I also want to note that none of the writing here has been generated by large language model, although you may have noticed this yourself by the proliferation of spelling mistakes.
